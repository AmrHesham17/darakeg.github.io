About Us

Our Mission & Vision

To deliver premium, halal-certified meat from Egypt to global markets with unwavering commitment to quality, food safety, and ethical practices — under our trusted Nour Land brand.

To become the world’s most trusted source for halal meat exports, setting benchmarks in quality, sustainability, and food integrity while promoting Egypt as a leader in ethical meat production.
